Vision
AdoLina is a next-generation logistics and shipping platform designed to streamline freight comparison, booking, and management through a secure and scalable full-stack solution. The platform offers real-time courier rate comparison, one-click booking, shipment tracking, and smart customs automation within a single digital experience. Utilizing API-driven integrations, data-driven decision making, and automation, AdoLina aims to facilitate faster, smarter, and more efficient movement of goods while reducing costs and manual effort.
Objectives
• Reduce manual effort and operational costs
• Enable API-driven integrations for carriers and customs
• Deliver a frictionless digital experience for shippers and logistics teams
• Provide instant freight intelligence and automated compliance
Core Features
1. Real-time Rate Comparison Engine
